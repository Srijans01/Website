About School


This school is located at Jugaldi Village, Uttarkashi. Mr. Gajendra Singh, qualified
educator from the same village is heading the school and is responsible for the overall administration of the school. He along with two other teachers are taking classes for these kids as well. Again, teaching english properly is a limitation for all three of them and hence, volunteers are sought to compensate for the same. A basic infrastructure consisting of 4 rooms along with Office/toilet facility is available. We also have a computer available in premises for performing the IT functions of the school.

About Arunoday Foundation


Arunoday Foundation was formed/registered on 25th of Feb 2016. The mission of the foundation is to provide quality education to the rural children. The inspiration comes from Tapovani Mata Sant Subhadra’s drive to spread ‘Shiksha, Swasthya and Sanskar’. The founder Narendra Shetty and General Secretary Gajendra Singh have been followers of Tapovani Mata for a long time.
